Images & Media

In this lesson, you'll learn how to embed images, audio, and video into your HTML pages. Multimedia elements help enhance user engagement.

Covered Tags:

  • <img> – for images
  • <audio> – for sound
  • <video> – for videos
  • Attributes like src, alt, controls

Image Example:

<img src="images/html-logo.png" alt="HTML Logo" width="200" />

Audio Example:

<audio controls>
  <source src="media/sound.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
</audio>

Video Example:

<video controls width="320">
  <source src="media/video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>
