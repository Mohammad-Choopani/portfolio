Accessibility

Accessibility means building websites that are usable by everyone â€” including people with disabilities.

1. Why It Matters

  • Blind users may rely on screen readers.
  • Color-blind users may struggle with poor contrast.
  • Motor-impaired users may use keyboard-only navigation.

2. Best Practices

  • Use semantic HTML (like <nav>, <main>, <article>).
  • Add alt text to images for screen readers.
  • Ensure good color contrast between text and background.
  • Enable keyboard navigation (e.g. using tabindex).
  • Use ARIA labels where necessary.

3. Example

<img src="logo.png" alt="Company Logo">

<button aria-label="Open menu">
  <i class="fas fa-bars"></i>
</button>
